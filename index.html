About the competiton

Explore a new kind of coding competition - CodeRun; is a free online IT competition , which will take place between April 11th-18th , 2022. This unique team based competition offers students that are passionate about coding the opportunity to put their knowledge into practice, by participating in various software programming challenges, where they can win amazing prizes.

The first part of the event (April 11th-14th) will consist of several training sessions offered by our partner companies. These will focus on key programming topics and these sessions are delivered so everyone can understand what the topic is about, if you are a beginner, you’ve found the right place to learn new skills.
The second part (April 15th-18th) of CodeRun; will be our coding competition.

The competition has a cylindrical structure, consisting of different tests offered by companies and one organized by BEST Cluj-Napoca, with 2 levels of difficulty. These are based on various topics: debugging, functional programming, object oriented programming or another topic proposed by the company. Our teams will be able to choose the desired level of difficulty and will go through all the stages that are focused on different areas of coding. Following their achievement, they will be awarded points, the maximum score being the winner.
